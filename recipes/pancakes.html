Pancakes

Pancakes are like cozy hugs on a plate for breakfast. They're soft, fluffy circles of happiness that taste amazing with syrup, butter, or fruit.
You can make them sweet or savory, depending on what you like. Pancakes bring everyone together, whether it's a lazy morning at home
or a big brunch with friends. They're just simple, delicious treats that make any day better!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 60ml castor sugar
  • 175ml milk
  • 250ml cake flour
  • 10 ml baking powder
  • 1ml salt
  • 15 ml oil, plus extra for cooking
  • Butter and honey to serve
