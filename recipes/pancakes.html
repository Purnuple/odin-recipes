Pancakes

Pancakes are like cozy hugs on a plate for breakfast. They're soft, fluffy circles of happiness that taste amazing with syrup, butter, or fruit.
You can make them sweet or savory, depending on what you like. Pancakes bring everyone together, whether it's a lazy morning at home
or a big brunch with friends. They're just simple, delicious treats that make any day better!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 60ml castor sugar
  • 175ml milk
  • 250ml cake flour
  • 10ml baking powder
  • 1ml salt
  • 15ml oil, plus extra for cooking
  • Butter and honey to serve

Steps:

  1. Whisk the egg in a bowl.Add the castor sugar and mix well.
  2. Add half of the milk(75ml) and all the oil to the egg mixture. Mix well.
  3. Sift the flour, baking powder and salt together in another bowl, and then add to
    the egg mixture. Stir with a wooden spoon until smooth.
  4. Add the rest of milk slowly and mix well.
  5. Grease the frying pan with oil and heat over medium heat. Keep the serving you're going to use warm.
  6. Drop small amounts of batter into the pan. When bubbles form on the surface, turn and cook
    on the other side until browned. Remove from the pan and keep warm while you use up the rest of batter.
  7. Serve with butter and honey.
